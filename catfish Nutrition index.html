GBFish

Nutritional content

GB Smoked catfish is your good source of protein, omega-3 fatty acids, and minerals like calcium, magnesium, and zinc. It's also a good source of vitamins B6 and B12, and potassium and selenium.

Protein: GB Smoked catfish is high in protein. 57g serving of smoked dried striped catfish fillet contains 40g of protein.

Omega-3 fatty acids: GB Smoked catfish contains omega-3 fatty acids, which are healthy fats that can help reduce inflammation and improve heart health.

Minerals: GB Smoked catfish contains minerals like calcium, magnesium, and zinc.

Vitamins: GB Smoked catfish contains vitamins B6 and B12.

Potassium and selenium: GB Smoked catfish contains potassium and selenium.
